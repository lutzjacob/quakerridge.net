Quaker Ridge






The Quaker Ridge, located in Gramercy on the northeast corner of Third Avenue and 21st Street, is a post-war co-op with 263 apartments:
a mix of studios, one, two and three bedroom apartments, as well as some combined apartments. The building was built in 1963, and converted to a co-op in 1982.
